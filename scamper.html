On this stage, we take one of your brainstorm ideas (hover over 'This Idea:' on the navigation bar to see your full brainstorm idea) and we alter it according to the SCAMPER method. These altertations will then be used in the next stage to develop your original brainstorm idea and make it more creative.

So lets explain the SCAMPER method. For it to work effecively, you must try to input an idea into each SCAMPER letter cue.

For example, we click on the S letter, and the cue is Subsitute. Underneath, there will be a question to help us delve deeper into the Subsitute cue, such as "Can I use other ingrediants or materials?". So, if our problem was "How could we solve social media addiction?", and our brainstorm idea was "Put their phone in a wooden box", then I could create a new idea in the text-box based on this question "Put their phone in a glass box". Here I have subsited one element of my idea, and created a new, different idea.

You can change the questions by pressing "Change Question" if you find it confusing, or the question does not relate directly to your problem/goal.
